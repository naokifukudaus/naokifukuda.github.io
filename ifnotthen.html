If Not, Then

By NAOKI FUKUDA

If they don’t shoot me, they imprison me;
If they don’t imprison me, they encamp me;
If they don’t encamp me, they’re imperialist;

If they aren’t imperialists, they’re nationalists;
If they aren’t nationalists, they’re xenophobic;
If they aren’t xenophobic, they’re racist;
If they aren’t racist, they’re misogynistic;
If they aren’t misogynistic, they’re anti-queer;

If they aren’t any of those things, they stay silent about it; they stand by;
If they speak out about those things, it’s because they’re motivated by profit; they turn out to be capitalists in the end;

And you call me “radical.”
